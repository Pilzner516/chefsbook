The Story

Origin

Built for how cooks actually work.

Recipe apps have existed for years, but they've always asked too much of the cook. Copy and paste the recipe. Re-type the ingredients. Manually create your shopping list. Switch apps for your meal plan.

ChefsBook was built to remove every one of those friction points. Paste a URL and the recipe is structured and ready. Photograph a cookbook page and it's searchable in seconds. Describe a recipe out loud and it's saved and tagged before you reach the kitchen counter.

ChefsBook runs across web and Android (iOS coming), syncs in real time, works offline, and is built privacy-first on self-hosted infrastructure — your recipe collection never lives on a server at risk of being shut down or sold.

Key differentiator #1

Import anything.

Nine import methods including camera scan (single and multi-page), voice dictation, Instagram posts, YouTube videos, physical cookbooks by ISBN or photo, batch browser bookmark import, and URL paste with AI gap-filling. No other consumer recipe app is close on import breadth.

Key differentiator #2

Genuinely multilingual.

Not just the UI — the recipe content translates too. Every ingredient, step, and note, cached per recipe so it's always instant. Five languages: English, French, Spanish, Italian, German.

Key differentiator #3

AI that earns its keep.

Auto-tagging, smart purchase units for shopping, AI meal planning, dish identification from photos, content moderation, and recipe generation from a spoken description. AI is woven throughout — not a single bolt-on feature.

Social & Community

Following

Follow chefs, not algorithms.

Follow any public user. What's New feed shows a chronological stream of their recipe uploads — no promoted content, no ranking. Follower/following counts on all profiles.

Comments & likes

Messaging & sharing

Private DMs with real-time Supabase sync and AI moderation. Share recipes via link (with attribution tracking), print, PDF (Pro), or social caption generation for Instagram / Pinterest / Facebook (Pro).

Technology

AI

Claude API (Anthropic)

Sonnet for complex extraction, generation, and translation. Haiku for classification, moderation, tagging, and purchase units. Cost-optimised per function — AI features available on all paid plans without premium pricing.

Platform

Web · Android · Chrome

Next.js 15 web app at chefsbk.app. Expo SDK 54 native Android app on Google Play (iOS in development). Chrome/Edge extension (MV3). All three apps share a single Turborepo monorepo with shared database, AI, and UI packages — one codebase, three platforms.

Download: Google Play · com.chefsbook.app
Infrastructure

Self-hosted, privacy-first

Self-hosted Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth + Storage + Realtime). Cloudflare Tunnel for public access. No third-party SaaS dependencies for core data. User recipes never stored on external servers.
